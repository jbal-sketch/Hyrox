Training Notes

  • Runna Runs: Follow your Runna plan exactly - focus on tempo work and intervals to bring 5K time down to 24:00-24:30
  • Home Weights: Train slightly heavier than race weights to build capacity (e.g., 5-6kg thrusters vs 4kg wall ball)
  • Gym Sessions: Practice exact race weights and transitions. Film yourself to check technique.
  • Wall Balls: Biggest priority - practice 2-3x per week. Work up to sets of 25-30 unbroken.
  • Burpee Broad Jumps: Practice 3x per week at home. Focus on efficiency: chest-to-ground, explosive jump.
  • Transitions: Practice running straight from treadmill to station. No rest between run finish and station start.
  • Recovery: Don't skip rest days. Recovery is when you adapt and get stronger.
